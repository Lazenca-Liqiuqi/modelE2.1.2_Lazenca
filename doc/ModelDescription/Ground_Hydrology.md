# Ground Hydrology / 地下水文学

The soils are modelled separately for bare and vegetated soil. Additionally, there is a canopy layer over the vegetated portion.
土壤按裸土和植被覆盖土壤分别建模。此外，在植被部分上方存在冠层。

The soils are modelled using 6 variable depth layers.
土壤使用6个可变深度层进行建模。

Evapotransiration takes water from the surface pools, and from below as a function of rooting depths.
蒸散发从地表蓄水与下部土层取水，其取水深度是根系深度的函数。

Runoff is calculated using a TOPmodel approach.
径流使用TOPmodel方法计算。
